# One Bill

**Live app:** https://muffcruster420-bot.github.io/one-bill/

Stop fighting over the check. One Bill splits by what you *actually* ate, not "everyone pays $27."

---

## How to use (30 seconds)

1. **Enter the bill total** – just the food + drinks subtotal, before tax and tip
2. **Set tax % and tip %** – defaults are 0% tax, 18% tip (tap to change)
3. **Add people**
   - Tap "+ Add person" for each person
   - Type their name
   - **Weight = how much they ate** (1 = average, 1.5 = ate 50% more, 0.7 = light eater)
4. **Check the boxes** for each person:
   - 🍹 = had drinks
   - 🥗 = had apps/shared plates  
   - 🍰 = had dessert
   - Leave unchecked if they skipped it
5. **Tap "SPLIT IT"** – you'll get exact amounts to the penny

### Example
Bill is $100, 13% tax, 20% tip
- Jesse: weight 1, had drinks and apps
- Sam: weight 1.5, had everything
- Alex: weight 0.8, no drinks

Tap SPLIT IT → Jesse pays $38.12, Sam pays $62.45, Alex pays $28.03 (adds up to $128.60 total with tax/tip)

---

## Tips to avoid mistakes

- **Don't include tax/tip in the first box** – the app adds them for you
- **Weight is not a percentage** – just use 1 for normal, adjust up/down
- **Uncheck boxes for people who skipped** – that's the whole point
- **Last person gets the penny adjustment** – so totals always match the receipt exactly
- **Works offline** – save the page to your home screen, no internet needed at the restaurant

## Why this is better than "split evenly"
If someone didn't drink, they shouldn't pay for your cocktails. If someone had a salad while you had steak, weights fix it without doing math.
